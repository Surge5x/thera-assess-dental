About Emerald Dental

Modern dental clinic in Tirana led by oral and maxillofacial surgeon Dr Loreta Qemali, treating around 1,000 patients a year from Albania, the UK, Germany, Italy and beyond. Offers implant dentistry, crowns and veneers (zirconia and E.max), bridges and dentures at prices roughly 60–70% below Western Europe, with complimentary airport and hotel transfers for international patients. Tirana is an emerging value destination for UK dental tourism.
