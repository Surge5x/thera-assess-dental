About KCM Clinic

Multispecialty private hospital heavily geared to medical tourists, with published all-inclusive orthopaedic packages (diagnostics, anaesthesia, hospital stay, rehab), hotel/transfer arrangements and translators. Surgeons have performed 4,500+ hip and knee replacements; also a major dental and bariatric destination for UK patients.
