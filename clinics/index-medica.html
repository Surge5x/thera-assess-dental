About Index Medica

Index Medica is a private dental clinic in central Krakow, Poland, focused on international dental travel patients from the UK, Ireland and beyond. It provides implants, bone grafting, sinus lifts, crowns, bridges, veneers, root canal treatment and cosmetic dentistry. The clinic has held ISO 9001 certification since 2008 and undergoes annual TÜV Rheinland audits, and is ranked among the top three dental clinics in Poland by the independent Global Clinic Rating. A four-person, fully English-speaking international patients department coordinates 24/7 assistance, airport pickup and hotel bookings at partner design hotels. Index Medica also offers pre-treatment consultations and follow-up in the UK and Ireland.
