About Bizrahmed (Bizrah Medical Center)

Bizrahmed is a long-established Dubai cosmetic-surgery group (operating roughly 30 years) founded by rhinoplasty and otolaryngology specialist Dr. Bashar Bizrah, with additional locations in Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, London and Jeddah. It is DHA-regulated and best known for facial plastic surgery and rhinoplasty, but offers full body and breast aesthetic surgery. Its surgeon roster is unusually senior for the region and includes internationally recognised names such as Mr. Norman Waterhouse, a Harley Street plastic surgeon and past President of both BAAPS and ISAPS. As a Dubai clinic it sits at a premium price point versus Turkey, but offers UK/EU-comparable oversight, English-language service and short recovery-friendly luxury infrastructure.
