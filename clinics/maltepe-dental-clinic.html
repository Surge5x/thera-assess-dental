About Maltepe Dental Clinic

Maltepe Dental Clinic is an Istanbul clinic in the Kadikoy district that has treated international patients for over two decades, reporting more than 14,000 patients from 85+ countries. It is notable for a UK-facing team led by chief dentist Dr. Yusuf Ilhan, who is registered with the UK General Dental Council, alongside a dentist who is a member of the American Dental Association. The clinic publishes clear multi-currency pricing (GBP, EUR, USD) for implants, E.max and zirconium restorations and whitening, and holds several ISO certifications plus Turkey's Health Tourism Certificate. It offers virtual consultations, accommodation and airport transfers, plus a 10-year guarantee on implant treatment and a lifetime materials guarantee.
