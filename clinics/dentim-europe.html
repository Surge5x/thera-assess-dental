About Dentim Europe

One of the largest and most modern dental practices in Poland, combining a clinic with an on-site dental laboratory and implant-parts centre in Katowice. Specialises in implant-based reconstruction (developed a 1-day implant reconstruction method in 2010) and cosmetic dentistry, and is a long-standing choice for UK dental-tourism patients. Ranked by GCR among the leading clinics in Poland for international reputation.
