About Hermest Hair Clinic

Hermest Hair Clinic is an Istanbul (Üsküdar) hair transplant centre offering Sapphire FUE, DHI and its proprietary Unique FUE® technique. The clinic uses flat-rate, all-inclusive package pricing (up to 4,000 grafts) rather than per-graft fees, with each package covering surgery, accommodation, transfers, PRP and aftercare. It markets heavily to international patients with English and German support and partner contact points in London, Miami and Berlin. Its multidisciplinary team spans hair restoration, dermatology and plastic surgery, and it applies an internal 'All-In Safety' protocol standardising the procedure from extraction to aftercare. Hermest is certified by Turkey's Ministry of Health and Ministry of Tourism, reports Google ratings around 4.9, and offers a written lifetime warranty certificate.
