About Myra Dental Centre

Myra Dental Centre is an Antalya-based dental clinic with a dedicated UK-facing operation and a 24/7 English-speaking coordinator service. It reports around 25 years of clinical experience and more than 27,000 patients treated, with a specialist team of six dentists covering implantology, prosthodontics, orthodontics and aesthetic dentistry. The clinic publishes a transparent GBP price list and works with premium implant systems including Straumann and CAMLOG alongside E.max and Celtra Duo veneers. It holds Turkey's International Health Tourism Authorization Certificate and markets savings of roughly 50-70% versus UK prices. Antalya's short airport transfer times and resort setting make it a popular combined treatment-and-recovery destination for British patients.
