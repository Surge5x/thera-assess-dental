About Evergreen Dental

Evergreen Dental is a Hungarian-American dental clinic in central Budapest, located at 45 Andrássy út within the city's UNESCO World Heritage zone in a restored 1870s building. It serves both Hungarian and international patients, providing English-language communication from first contact through aftercare. Services span aesthetic fillings, crowns, veneers, root canal treatment, orthodontics, oral surgery, bone grafting and dental implants using brands including Nobel Biocare, Straumann, Megagen and Alpha Bio. The clinic was the first in Hungary to offer Digital Smile Design and offers treatment under general anaesthesia for anxious patients. For overseas patients it provides travel management support including airport pickup and hotel arrangements.
