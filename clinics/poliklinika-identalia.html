About Poliklinika Identalia

Poliklinika Identalia is a dental clinic in Zagreb, Croatia, located at Petrovaradinska 1 and described on its own site as one of the largest dental clinics in Zagreb. It offers implantology, full-arch rehabilitation, cosmetic and restorative dentistry, and treats international patients, with a dedicated focus on full-mouth solutions such as All-on-4 and All-on-6. The clinic works with Nobel Biocare and Straumann implant systems and offers intravenous sedation under anaesthesiologist supervision. Identalia coordinates care for overseas patients, providing accommodation and transport assistance, free online consultations and WhatsApp support. Its team reports having completed more than 30,000 dental implants.
