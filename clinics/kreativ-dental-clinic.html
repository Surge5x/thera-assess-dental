About Kreativ Dental Clinic

Kreativ Dental Clinic is a large purpose-built dental clinic in Budapest, Hungary, established in 1998 and focused on international patients seeking dental implants and cosmetic dentistry. Its team includes implantologists, oral surgeons, endodontists and general practitioners, supported by an in-house laboratory whose master ceramists produce crowns, bridges and veneers. Services span single implants, full-arch restorations, bone grafting, sinus lifts, periodontal surgery, porcelain and zirconia crowns, and e.max veneers. The clinic uses CBCT scanning, digital measurement and microscope-assisted dentistry. It markets heavily to UK and other European patients, citing savings of up to roughly 50-70% versus UK prices, and operates a UK-facing coordination and aftercare service.
